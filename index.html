1.Flying squirrel

Interesting characteristics of the flying squirrel

  1. Although they are known as 'fluing squirrels' they do not actually fly - they glide through the air
  2. These squirrels are nocturnal. They can collect up to 15000 nuts per season
  3. They reside in holes made in trees by the woodpecker
